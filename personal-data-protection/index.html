Personal data protection

At Royal Defence Technologies, we recognize the importance of protecting personal data and ensuring the privacy and security of our website visitors, customers, and partners. This Personal Data Protection page outlines our commitment to handling and safeguarding personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws and regulations. We strive to maintain the highest standards of data protection to earn and maintain your trust. Please take a moment to read through this page to understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

  1. Collection and Use of Personal Data:

  • We collect personal data, such as names, contact information, and job titles, only for specific purposes relevant to our business activities.
  • Personal data is obtained through voluntary submission, such as when filling out contact forms or subscribing to our newsletter.
  • We use personal data solely for the purposes communicated to you at the time of collection, such as responding to inquiries, providing requested information, and delivering relevant updates.

  1. Data Security Measures:

  • We have implemented robust technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data.
  • Our systems and processes are designed to protect against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure of personal information.
  • We regularly review and update our security practices to adapt to evolving threats and maintain the integrity of personal data.

  1. Data Retention and Storage:

  • We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
  • Personal data is stored in secure databases and servers, ensuring appropriate safeguards are in place to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

  1. Third-Party Data Sharing:

  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.
  • In certain cases, we may engage trusted third-party service providers to assist in our business operations. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality obligations and are authorized to process personal data only as necessary to deliver the requested services.

  1. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

  • Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content.
  • You have the option to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that certain website features may be affected by disabling cookies.

  1. Your Rights and Choices:

  • You have the right to access, update, and correct your personal data held by us.
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the email or contacting us directly.
  • If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal data, please reach out to our Data Protection Officer using the contact information provided on this page.

  1. Compliance with Data Protection Laws:

  • We comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.
  • We continuously review and update our data protection practices to ensure compliance and maintain the highest standards of privacy and security.
